Marriott International - Bethesda, Maryland

About Marriott International

Offering awesome views over the Tête d'Or Park, this four star hotel features modern accommodation and upscale amenities. The main Lyon Saint Exupery airport is 18.6 miles away. Contemporary rooms feature private marble bathrooms with bathrobes and slippers. Conveniences include an MP3 with radio, coffee-maker and flat screen TV. Upscale rooms offer private balconies and lounge access.
The executive Lounge features wireless Internet access at Marriott Lyon Cité Internationale, a theatre-style venue can accommodate up to 600 guests, or unwind after a long day in the fully-fitted fitness centre.
Start the day off with a buffet breakfast at Marriott Lyon Cité Internationale, then enjoy Italian food at the on-site restaurant, Zucca. The outdoor terrace serves drinks with beautiful views of the River Rhone.
Relax at Parc de la Tête d'Or, a popular park with locals and visitors, then pop in to the zoo, or stroll down to the Musée d'art Contemporain de Lyon, three minutes away.
