The Lalit - New Delhi, India

About The Lalit New Delhi

An imposing hi-rise particularly well known for its dining and nightlife, The Lalit New Delhi is located in central Delhi, just one kilometre's walk from the greenery of Central Park. The 461 rooms and suites boast a contemporary design. hardwood floors, and amenities such as large flat-screen TVs, entertainment systems, free Wi-Fi, and minibars. A walk-in closet and work desk allow guests to keep both their clothes and papers in order. Guests here can break a sweat in the gym, enjoy pampering at Rejuve Spa, and feast their eyes on works in Delhi's only hotel art gallery. If none of this appeals, soaking up the sun on the pool terrace or shopping in the luxury arcade also await. One of three restaurants, Baluchi offers Indian curries in a sophisticated venue. The pastry chef at The Lalit Boulangerie creates delectable pastries, and 24/7 Bar mixes drinks without considering the time of day. Delhi's iconic Red Fort is about a six-kilometre drive north of The Lalit New Delhi.
