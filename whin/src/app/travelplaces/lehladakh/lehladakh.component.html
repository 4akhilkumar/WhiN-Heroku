Leh-Ladakh – The Biker’s Paradise

By Air

Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport in Leh is the airport connecting the region to other places by air.

By Rail

Jammu Tawi Railway Station (700 km from Ladakh) is the nearest rail head. From here, one can hire a cab or board a JKSRTC bus to Ladakh.

By Road

The most popular means of reach Ladakh remains to be road. Travelers can go on a bike/jeep ride to the destination. Bike trips from Delhi, Chandigarh, and Manali are most popular.

Attractions

Zanskar Valley, Pangong Tso Lake, Khardung-La Pass, Spituk Gompa, and Hemis National Park

Best time to visit

April to mid-May and mid-September to mid-October
